# Vaedrin — The Witness

*DM eyes only. Do not share with players.*

---

## The Concept

A character who appears across multiple campaigns run by different DMs. He is not a villain, not a quest-giver, not a savior. He is a collector of moments — he arrives near historically significant events, observes, and disappears. He has been doing this longer than anyone in any campaign has been alive.

Players should not catch on for several campaigns. Very little should ever be learned about him.

---

## Real Name

**Vaedrin**

Ancient. From no recognizable language. This name should not be revealed until 5+ campaigns in. When it finally comes out, attentive players may notice it is an anagram of the name he used in the Strange Times campaign — which is the first retroactive clue that all his appearances are the same person.

---

## The One Sacred Rule (All DMs Must Follow)

> He is always eating or drinking something wrong for the context.

Fresh fruit in a dungeon. Hot tea in a desert. Plain bread at a royal feast. Persimmons underground. He never explains it. He never comments on it. If asked, he deflects warmly and changes the subject.

This is the only detail that must be consistent across every campaign. Everything else — name, appearance, role — can vary.

---

## Strange Times Identity

**Name:** Davan

**Role:** A roaming archivist who collects records of places that no longer exist. He carries a leather-bound journal that looks too old for its condition. He is at the Festival of the Forgotten because *"collecting forgotten things is what I do."* He has papers that look official but reference no organization anyone can verify.

**The food:** A persimmon. Underground, at the Festival. No one sells persimmons here.

---

## Behavioral Rules

1. Always at the edge of scenes — never at the center
2. Asks one question he already knows the answer to
3. Says one true thing players will only understand later (casual, not dramatic)
4. Never stays — always just leaving or just arrived
5. Never fights — if initiative is called in a scene he's in, he is not there when it starts
6. Survives everything by simply not being in danger's path
7. Never lies outright — but omits enormous amounts

---

## Strange Times Scenes

### Scene 1 — Festival of the Forgotten *(primary appearance)*

He sits apart from the crowd, writing in his journal. If approached:

- Introduces himself as Davan, an archivist
- Asks one player: *"What's the last thing you remember clearly — before all this?"* Listens with unsettling focus
- Says casually: *"This is the third facility like this one. The first two ended... less cleanly."* Changes subject immediately if pressed
- When leaving, presses a coin into the nearest player's hand: *"You'll want to hold onto that. Not sure why yet."*
- Gone

### Scene 2 — Area 7 campfire *(optional, DM discretion)*

He is at the edge of firelight, seemingly asleep against a wall. After a player shares their last memory, he murmurs without opening his eyes: *"That's the version they wanted you to have."*

If anyone reacts, he appears to be fully asleep. He is gone before morning.

### Scene 3 — Underground river / caves *(travel encounter, optional)*

He is walking the opposite direction, completely unbothered. Nods at the party. If stopped:

*"Wrong tunnel for where you want to be. Right tunnel for where you'll need to be."*

Keeps walking.

---

## The Coin

Left with the players at the end of Scene 1.

- Copper, heavier than it should be
- One face: an eye with a vertical pupil
- Other face: worn completely smooth, no image
- No mint mark, no text
- Not magical by any detectable means

## Cross-Campaign Breadcrumb Schedule

| Campaign | Breadcrumb |
|---|---|
| 1 — Strange Times | References an unknown place by name, casually, no explanation. Drop one place name no one recognizes. |
| 2 | Same unusual snack spotted — players likely dismiss it |
| 3 | Uses the phrase *"that's a new one"* about something that is clearly ancient |
| 4 | A player who was also in Campaign 1 notices he hasn't aged at all |
| 5 | His full name is revealed to be an anagram of "Davan" + additional letters — pointing back to Vaedrin |

## DM Coordination

Keep a shared private doc (or this file) updated with:

1. **The tell** — the food/drink detail. Never changes.
2. **Current surface name** — update after each campaign
3. **What he said and did** — log each appearance so future DMs can echo it
4. **The rule** — never helps directly, never harms, only witnesses

---

## What He Is Not

- Not a deity or demigod
- Not pulling strings behind the scenes
- Not interested in being recruited, hired, or befriended
- Not someone who gives quests or delivers prophecy
- Not someone who can be charmed, intimidated, or cornered into answers
